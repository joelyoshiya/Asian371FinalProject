Dehua Porcelain: "White from China"

Dehua, found in Fujian China, is a historic site famous for the production of a special type of porcelain, referred to French as "Blanc De Chine," or White of China. This white whare has a brilliant white sheen, and once glazened gives off a luster like ivory or white-jade. Often used to sculpt figurines, or smaller sculptures, various vessels have been shaped by this pure and clean clay of the region.

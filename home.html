Welcome!

My name is Joel, and I am a Computer Sciences and Japaanese major currently heading into my third year at UW-Madison! As part of my Asian 371 course, in which we study The Senses and Chinese Literature, I decided to create this simple site to celebrate and study pre-modern, Ming-Qing Chinese historic vessels. The selection will be from the Metropolitan Museum of Art's collection, and will include some additional peripheral content to enhance the experience, as well as my analysis. I hope you enjoy~
